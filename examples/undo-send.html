Hover the pill to pause the countdown. Watch the server deadline get pushed out while you hold, then snap back when you let go. Hit Undo, or let it reach zero and send.

What you're feeling: the countdown is just the client rendering an authoritative server-side timer. Hovering sends a postpone heartbeat that pushes the deadline ~20s out so it never fires while you're reading; releasing pulls it back to a short grace. Undo cancels the job; reaching zero sends it. The server is always the source of truth — close your laptop mid-hover and it still sends at the last deadline.
